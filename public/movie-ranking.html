The Best Movies According to Umair

My top 4 movies of all-time.

Twilight

I love Twilight because of its unique blend of romance and supernatural intrigue. The intense connection between Bella and Edward keeps me hooked every time.

The Revenent

I like The Revenant for its breathtaking cinematography and raw depiction of survival. The intensity of Hugh Glass's journey is both brutal and inspiring.

Perfume:The Story of Murderer

I love Perfume: The Story of a Murderer for its haunting atmosphere and unique storyline. The way it explores obsession with scent is both chilling and mesmerizing

Benjamin Button

I like The Curious Case of Benjamin Button for its emotional depth and imaginative storytelling. The journey of aging in reverse is both poignant and thought-provoking
